Privacy Policy

Deadman's Wallet is a decentralized application that operates entirely on the Aptos blockchain. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. All wallet interactions happen directly between your wallet and the blockchain.

Your wallet address, transaction history, and asset information are public on the blockchain, but we have no access to your private keys or personal data.

Use at your own risk. This is a demonstration project for educational purposes.
